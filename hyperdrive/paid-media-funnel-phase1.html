How a funnel works, in plain terms. Awareness creates demand and fills the audience pool (the demand-gen video). Intent catches people already searching (Google) and warm prospects (LinkedIn document ads). Remarketing converts the warm audience the first two build - site visitors, video viewers and playbook downloaders. Remarketing is the piece most missing today, and it is where accreditation proof does the heavy lifting.

Funnel 1 - AI Training ($5,500/mo). Lower ticket and transactional, so it is the faster path to visible results. Now weighted toward the top: $2,000 of demand-gen video, a doubled intent layer ($1,000 LinkedIn document ads + $1,000 Google search), and $1,500 of accreditation-led remarketing.

Funnel 2 - Business Transformation ($3,500/mo). High ticket with a longer cycle, kept funded in parallel and adds a lead-magnet step before intent. Trimmed this round: lead magnet to $1,000, Business Performance search to $500, and the strategy-session remarketing to $1,000.

Brand layer ($1,000/mo). Branded Search on Google captures the high-intent demand both funnels generate, protecting it at the moment of search. Always-on, shown as the dashed band linking into both columns.

Phase 1 total: $10,000/mo - $5,500 + $3,500 + $1,000.
