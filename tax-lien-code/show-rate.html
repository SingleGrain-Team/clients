What to do with this
04Keep AI Flyer on a hard budget cap
AI / impersonal flyer creative is a volume filler, not a show-rate driver: Columbia CAE - AI Event Flyer Static 7.2% (n=690); DC DCA - AI Event Flyer Static 9.2% (n=619). History shows it collapses to 4-7% when over-funded (PHL n=404, IND n=282). Cap it at ~150-200 regs per market and protect budget for host-led creative.
05BOFU retargeting is doing its job; keep the warm-audience loop funded
Meta BOFU lifted show rate over TOFU in both markets (Columbia 12.0% BOFU vs 8.1% TOFU; DC 11.6% vs 8.0%). Reuse the winning TOFU static as the retargeting creative (the proven Blue Event Dates pattern) and keep the "Still Thinking About The Event?" and Brian "Are You Coming" retargeting cuts in rotation.
06Paired-market design held: read creative against geo, not in isolation
Columbia and DC ran a mirrored creative set in the same week, which lets the head-to-head table separate creative effect from market effect (2 TOFU-Meta concepts ran in both). Where a concept wins in both geos it is a real creative signal worth scaling; where it wins in one only, treat it as market-specific and re-test before generalizing. Continue the paired-market structure on the next cycle.
07Reuse the Workshop-Buyers LAL audience stack
Where the audience seed was tagged, the LAL Workshop-Buyers + Customer Match + Affinity Match stack has beaten interest-based targeting by 3-4pts on the same creative pool in DEN/SAN/LOU. Confirm the audience split is firing in the CAE/DCA campaign naming (several recent markets dropped audience tokens from utm_campaign) and default new prospecting to the Workshop-Buyers stack.
