⟲ Speed 1 — Evergreen NEVER pauses
The prospecting engine stays on at all times. It holds the Demand Gen learning and keeps the pool warm. Pausing it destroys the efficiency that makes $100K fit. Geo = only metros with an event in ≤90 days (rolling list), so there's always an open registration to optimize toward.
⏻ Speed 2 — Event layer is the TOGGLE
The bottom-of-funnel groups stay pre-built and paused. Switch on ~18 days before the event (E-18), switch off ~3 days after (E+3). Learning cost is low because it's remarketing to an already-warm pool and small audiences. This is the answer to "build everything in groups and just activate them."
